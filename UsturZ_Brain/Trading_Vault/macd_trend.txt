Strategy Name: MACD Trend Follower
Type: trend
Indicators: MACD, Bollinger Bands
Timeframe: 1h/4h
Rules:
- Buy when MACD signal line crosses above zero and price is at the lower Bollinger Band.
- Sell when MACD signal line crosses below zero and price is at the upper Bollinger Band.
- Risk/Reward: 1:2.
